Introduction:¶

This project is part of Udacity Data Analyst Nanodegree program. The dataset is focused on wrangling (and analyzing and visualizing) tweet archive on Twitter user @dog_rates also known as WeRateDogs which is Twitter account. The main goal is rating for people's dogs with humorous comments. Rating is many times higher than 10/10. Why? Because "they're good dogs Brent." WeRateDogs has over 4 million followers and has received international media coverage.

Retweet over Date¶

Graf and analyze is from data which were collected from November of 2015 untill September of 2019. From the graf we can see, that popularity is rising over time. The majority of the data is around 10 000 retweet every day and the most popular days have has from 40 000 to 80 000 retweets.

Retweet over Dating¶

We have same data as we used in a graf above. For the second graf we focused on how rating numerator has influence on number of retweets. We had one rule, our rating numerator couldn't be higher than 20 points. We can see very interesting thing, if we have rating numer around 1 point which is low we have more retweets as usual. Change is comming with 10 or more points where number of retweet has exponential growth from few thousands to thens of thousands. So if you want more retweets than usual try to have an ugly dog with a very low score 😊 or have to be very funny with numerator higher than of 10/10 points.
